Dish Description:

Filipino noodle soup made with thin, transparent mung bean noodles (sotanghon) and shredded chicken, simmered in a savory broth flavored with garlic, onions, and sometimes annatto for color. Often enriched with vegetables like carrots and cabbage and topped with toasted garlic and green onions, this warm and hearty dish is perfect for rainy days or special gatherings.
