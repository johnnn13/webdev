Ginataang Bilo-bilo with Langka

Dish Description:

Ginataang Bilo-Bilo is a Filipino dessert made with glutinous rice balls (bilo-bilo) cooked in sweetened coconut milk. It typically includes ingredients like sweet potatoes, bananas, jackfruit, and tapioca pearls, creating a creamy and comforting treat.

Dish Recipes:


  • 20 pieces Glutinous rice balls (Bilo-bilo)
  • 20 oz Jackfruit ripe (Sliced)
  • 2 cups Water
  • 2 cups Coconut cream
  • 3/4 cup Granulated white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups Tapioca pearls (Cooked)

Instructions:


  1. Pour the water into a cooking pot. Bring to a boil.

  2. Add the coconut cream. Stir and cook until the mixture starts to boil again.

  3. Gradually stir in the sugar.

  4. Add the ripe jackfruit. Cover and cook on low heat for 15 minutes.

  5. Add-in the bilo-bilo. Continue to cook for another 15 minutes while stirring every 3 minutes.

  6. Put in the sago. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes.

  7. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve.

  8. Share and enjoy!
