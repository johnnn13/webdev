Pininyahang Manok

Dish Description:

Pininyahang Manok is a Filipino dish featuring tender chicken simmered in a creamy, slightly sweet sauce made with pineapple, milk, and vegetables. The combination of juicy pineapple chunks and savory flavors creates a rich, refreshing taste, making it a comforting and delicious meal.

Dish Recipes:


  • 2 lbs. chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1 Knorr Chicken Cube
  • 8 ounces pineapple chunks
  • 1 carrot thinly (sliced crosswise)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced into squares)
  • 1 green bell pepper (sliced into squares)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons liver spread
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 5 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste



Instructions:


  1. Heat oil in a pan. Pan-fry the chicken pieces until the sides turn light brown in color. Remove from the chicken and set aside.
  2. Saut√© onion and garlic using the remaining oil.
  3. Add the chicken back in once the onion softens.
  4. Pour soy sauce and stir while deglazing the chicken residue stuck on the pan.
  5. Add pineapple chunks and water. Let boil.
  6. Put the Knorr Chicken Cube. Cover the pan. Adjust the heat between low to medium settings. Cook the chicken for 25 minutes.
  7. Add liver spread and carrot. Cook for 5 minutes.
  8. Add bell peppers. Season with salt and ground black pepper.
  9. Combine cornstarch with 3 tablespoons of water. Mix well and then pour into the pan. Stir until the sauce reaches your desired thickness.
  10. Add sugar. Cook for a minute.
  11. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve warm with rice.
  12. Share and enjoy!
