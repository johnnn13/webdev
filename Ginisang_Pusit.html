Ginisang Pusit

Dish Description:

This flavorful seafood dish features tender squid (pusit) sautéed with garlic, onions, and tomatoes, then simmered in its own ink or a savory soy sauce blend. The squid’s naturally briny taste pairs perfectly with the rich, slightly tangy sauce, resulting in a bold and satisfying dish.

Dish Recipes:


  • 1 lb. squid (cleaned and sliced)
  • 1 carrot (sliced diagonally)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced into squared)
  • 1 green bell pepper (sliced into squares)
  • 2 stalks leeks (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 thumb ginger (minced)
  • 1 ½ tablespoons cooking wine
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Ground white pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons baking soda



Instructions:


  1. Combine the sauce ingredients in a bowl. Stir until well blended. Set aside.

  2. Heat cooking oil in a wok. Sauté the carrot, onion, white part of the leek, garlic, ginger, and bell peppers for 1 minute.

  3. Add the squid. Stir-fry for 1 ½ minutes.

  4. Pour the cooking wine. Toss.

  5. Add the sauce. Stir and cook for 30 seconds.

  6. Season with ground white pepper.

  7. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with rice.

  8. Share and enjoy!
